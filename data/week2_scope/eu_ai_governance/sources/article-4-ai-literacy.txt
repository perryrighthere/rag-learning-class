Annex V Annex VI Annex VII Annex VIII Annex IX Annex X Annex XI Annex XII Annex XIII Chapter I: General Provisions Article 4: AI literacy Summary Providers and deployers of AI systems should take measures to ensure a sufficient level of AI literacy of their staff and other persons dealing with the operation and use of AI systems on their behalf. They should do so by taking into account their technical knowledge, experience, education and training as well as the context the AI systems are to be used in and the persons on whom the AI systems are to be used. The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding. Providers and deployers of AI systems shall take measures to ensure, to their best extent, a sufficient level of AI literacy of their staff and other persons dealing with the operation and use of AI systems on their behalf, taking into account their technical knowledge, experience, education and training and the context the AI systems are to be used in, and considering the persons or groups of persons on whom the AI systems are to be used.
